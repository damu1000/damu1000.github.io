Bio

I'm a Graduate Research Assistant at the Scientific Computing and Imaging Institute at the University of Utah, working with Prof. Martin Berzins. Before joining the Ph.D. program at Utah, I worked as a Software Engineer at Wipro Technologies from 2006 to 2011, and later with Deloitte as a Senior Analyst and Technical Architect from 2011 to 2015. I obtained a B.E. in Computer Engineering in 2006 at the University of Pune. My research interests cover a range of areas in High Performance Computing, including massively parallel solutions for complex science problems, GPU Programming, multi-threading, performance portability across modern multi-core and many core architectures, performance optimizations, and resiliency.
