Hello World

Bio

I'm a Ph.D. candidate and Graduate Research Assistant at the Scientific Computing and Imaging Institute at the University of Utah, working with Prof. Martin Berzins from 2016. Before joining the Ph.D. program, I worked for 9 years in the software development industry - initially as a Software Engineer at Wipro Technologies from 2006 to 2011, and later as a Technical Architect with Deloitte from 2011 to 2015.

Research

My research interests cover a range of areas in High Performance Computing (HPC) including:

  • Development of efficient programming models for GPUs and multithreaded environments on the next generation supercomputers
  • Massively parallel solutions to solve the complex scientific problems
  • Performance portability across modern multi-core, many-core, and GPU architectures
  • Performance optimizations on GPUs and multi-threaded environments
  • Aasynchronous many task runtime systems
  • Resiliency

Visit the Research and Publications pages for more details and source codes.
