Hello World

Bio

I'm a Ph.D. candidate and Graduate Research Assistant at the Scientific Computing and Imaging Institute at the University of Utah, working with Prof. Martin Berzins from 2016. I worked for 9 years in the software development industry - initially as a Software Engineer at Wipro Technologies from 2006 to 2011, and later as a Technical Architect with Deloitte from 2011 to 2015, before joining the Ph.D. program. I obtained a B.E. in Computer Engineering in 2006 at the University of Pune, India.

Research

My research interests cover a range of areas in High Performance Computing (HPC), including utilization GPUs and multi-threading for performance gains, development of efficient programming models for GPU and/or multithreaded environments, massively parallel solutions for the complex scientific problems, performance portability across modern multi-core and many core architectures, performance optimizations, asynchronous many task runtime systems, and resiliency. Check the Research and Publications pages for more details and source codes.
